Classification of English words in terms of their difficulty levels. We are expected to classify English words in terms of their difficult levels. The data set include 11,998 English words which has 12 classes: 1(easy) to 12(difficult). Feature Engineering is carried out to search effective features that well represent difficulty levels of the English words. Then, Support Vector Machine (SVM) finds decision boundary of the classification problem.

Support Vector Machine (SVM)

SVM: RBF Kernel function 
 SVC
Features:
- Length
- Vowel ration to the length of word
- Popularity from google search result
- Frequency appeared on Gutenberg corporal
- Position of Speech

Demo: write down a short English sentence and it will return difficulty level of individual words.
